Our story

About The Corner Grill

A small neighborhood restaurant built around grilled plates, honest pricing, and a dining room that feels calm instead of rushed.

Who we are

Good food without the noise

The Corner Grill began as a small local spot with a simple goal: good food, fair price, and a calm place for people to eat together.

We keep the menu focused so guests can quickly pick favorites. Most plates are grilled, seasonal, and meant to be shared after work or on a slow weekend afternoon.

Timeline

How we got here

2018

A corner booth idea

The Corner Grill started as a weekend pop-up with a short grill menu and a handful of regulars.

2021

The menu took shape

We narrowed the list to grilled plates, bowls, and a few desserts guests kept ordering again.

Today

Still keeping it simple

Same goal as day one: fresh food, fair prices, and a calm room where people can actually talk.

What we believe

Three rules we do not break

01

Cook with restraint

Seasonal ingredients and a short menu so every plate gets attention.

02

Price it fairly

Neighborhood pricing without hidden upsells or confusing combos.

03

Make room for everyone

Quick lunch tables, slower dinners, and takeout that still feels cared for.
